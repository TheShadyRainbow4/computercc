About Us

Welcome to ComputerCare Co. Computer Repair, Rehabilitation & Recycling. We are dedicated to providing the best computer repair and recycling services. Our team of certified technicians brings years of experience in diagnosing and resolving a wide range of computer issues. We pride ourselves on our customer-centric approach, ensuring every client receives personalized service tailored to their specific needs.

Founded in 2010, Dr. Tech has been a trusted name in the community for over a decade. We believe in sustainable practices, which is why we offer comprehensive recycling services to help reduce electronic waste. Our mission is to make technology accessible and functional for everyone, while also caring for our planet.

Recycling

We collect old hardware / Computers that were previously thought to be destined for the trash. We ensure all data is securely formatted and deleted with client consent. Our recycling process is environmentally friendly, and we work with certified recycling partners to ensure that all materials are disposed of responsibly. By choosing our recycling services, you contribute to reducing electronic waste and promoting a sustainable future.
